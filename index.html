Mission

This site aims to become a compilation of javascript facepalm confessions.

Rules

The only one rule for now is that your story must be in first person. Other people mistakes are good for irony and tangential learning.

I whant this site to be a place to laugh of ourselves as we learn of our own mistakes.

Hopefully this will make new commers more confident by knowing that we are all humans after all.
